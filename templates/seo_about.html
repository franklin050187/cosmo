About the game - Cosmoteer: Starship Architect & Commander

Check the official website : Here

  • The game

    Create and simulate the starship of your dreams! Take command in epic battles, manage your crew, and explore a perilous galaxy. Play solo or with friends, test your ship in online PvP, and let your imagination soar to the stars!

    Design starship

    Design incredible starships with intuitive creation tools that are easy to learn yet challenging to master. Fully customize your ship's shape and layout, placing individual modules like weapons, engines, hallways, and crew quarters wherever you choose. Your ship's design is crucial to its survival, and every decision will affect its performance in battle. Easily share your creations with others through the Steam Workshop, forums, and Discord.

    Real-time tactical battles

    Command your ship or fleet in real-time tactical battles. Experience physics-driven, explosive, and immensely satisfying combat. Each module can be individually targeted and destroyed, with entire ships capable of breaking apart into multiple pieces. Disable an enemy's weapons to nullify its attack, destroy its shields to weaken its defenses, or deliver a decisive blow by taking out its reactor.
